# LODE Tokenomics

LODE is a privacy-centric cryptocurrency built as a decentralized Layer-1 chain. Its economic model is strictly defined to ensure absolute fairness and digital scarcity.

## Supply & Distribution

- **Total Maximum Supply:** 100,000,000 LODE (100 Million)
- **Decimals:** 8
- **Base Unit:** 1 grain ($10^{-8}$ LODE)
- **Premine:** 0 LODE
- **Developer Allocation / ICO:** 0 LODE (No ICO, no presale, no Dev fund)
- **Tail Emission:** None (Hard capped at 100,000,000 LODE)

*Every single LODE in existence must be mined through Proof of Work.*

## Emission Schedule (The Halving)

Unlike Monero's smooth emission curve, LODE utilizes a Bitcoin-style step halving model. 

- **Block Time:** 120 Seconds
- **Initial Block Reward:** 100 LODE
- **Halving Interval:** Every 500,000 Blocks (~1.9 Years)
